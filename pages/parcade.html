PARCADE is a transposition of the classic game PONG onto the cityscape. Players are invited to revisit the game in a new and unexpected way. Instead of controlling the game with the traditional PONG controllers, players control the game with seesaws! Two seesaws are placed in front of a projection of the game and control the two paddles, the position of each paddle is mapped to the angle of the seesaws. The two­ player game is then transformed into a four­ player game, 2 vs 2. To win, players have no choice but to cooperate. This a collaborative project made with my brother, he designed the seesaws and did all the hardware. I programmed both the pong game and the microcontrollers in the seesaws.
